Vietnamese American Entrepreneur Network

The immigrant experience is intimately tied with the entrepreneurial spirit. This informal online meeting place and meet up group is to promote and support Vietnamese Americans who have businesses, have friends and family who have businesses or want to start up a business.

We share our background, stories, experience, and the type of business we are interested in or are running, and the needs we are serving. All business stories and issues are welcome, from running a small shop to running a large company.

Contact us at vaendotorg@gmail.com
